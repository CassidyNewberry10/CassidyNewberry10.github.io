What is a gender wage gap?

"The gender pay gap or gender wage gap is the average difference between the remuneration for men and
women who are working. Women are generally considered to be paid less than men."

What is a gender unemployment gap?

The gender unemployment gap is a gap that fluctuates in the United States where at times women experience
higher rates of unemployment than men.
